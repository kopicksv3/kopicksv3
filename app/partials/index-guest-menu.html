USA ELIGIBILITY

The following states do not allow fantasy sports competition for cash: Arizona, Arkansas, Delaware, Iowa, Louisiana, Michigan, Montana, Tennessee, and Washington. Residents of Alabama and Nebraska must be 19 years of age to play in cash Daily Fantasy Sports contests.

If you fall outside these states and are still having trouble depositing, or have deposit questions in general please do not hesitate to contact us.

CANADA ELIGIBILITY

The following provinces do not allow fantasy sports competition for cash: Prince Edward Island and Quebec are not eligible to participate. Residents of British Columbia must be 19 years of age to play in cash Daily Fantasy Sports contests.

If you fall outside these provinces and are still having trouble depositing, or have deposit questions in general please do not hesitate to contact us.
