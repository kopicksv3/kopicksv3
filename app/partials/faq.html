Frequently Asked Questions

What is Knockout Picks?

Knockout Picks brings the greatest variety of games in the fantasy Sports world with a user-friendly experience that has become harder and harder to find.

With a great team of designers and developers we have created a site with endless amounts of unique games in daily, weekly, and season-long formats.

Not ready to play for some dough? We have great freerolls! You can also play one of our smaller free daily games or play a free game for bragging rights and earn points towards cash games and prizes!

Is Knockout Picks Legal?

Yes. Passed by the US Federal Government in 2006, the Unlawful Internet Gambling Enforcement Act included an exemption indicating legality of fantasy sports.

This exemption reads as follows:

"Participation in any fantasy or simulation sports game or educational game or contest in which (if the game or contest involves a team or teams) no fantasy or simulation team is based on the current membership of an actual team that is a member of an amateur or professional sports organization and meets the following conditions:

  1. All prizes and awards offered to winning participants are established and made known to the participants in advance of the game or contest and their value is not determined by the number of participants or the amount of any fees paid by participants.
  2. All winning outcomes reflect the relative knowledge and skill of the participants and are determined predominantly by accumulated statistical results of the performance of individuals (athletes in the case of sports events) in multiple real-world sporting or other events.
  3. No winning outcome is based: (a.) On the score, point spread, or any performance or performances of any single real world team or any combination of such teams; or (b.) Solely on any single performance of an individual athlete in any single real-world sporting or other event."

Fantasy sports have been determined a “game of skill” and are therefore not within the scope of the various U.S. gambling laws.

Feel free to look over our terms of use for any further info, or simply contact us with inquiries as we are always available!

States not falling under this law: Arizona, Arkansas, Delaware, Iowa, Louisiana, Michigan, Montana, Tennessee, and Washington. Residents of Prince Edward Island and Quebec are not eligible to participate in Knockout Picks cash games.

When do I Receive Winnings for Daily Contests?

Cash won in daily contests are distributed to all accounts within an hour after the last game is officially over -- providing that stats have become official.

What is a Freeroll?

A Freeroll is a daily/weekly tournament that is played for free, either offered by Knockout Picks as a tournament that any player can join, or as a tournament for players willing to use a certain amount of their Knockout points.

Prizes for freerolls can include cash, Knockout Points Prizes or entries into big cash tournaments.

How Do I Earn My Deposit Bonus?

Deposit bonuses are released into accounts at an incremental rate of 20% of the entry fee for whichever contest you have entered. Any deposit bonuses released into an account from the bonus section of their account is then available for withdrawal.

Do I Have to Pay Taxes on My Winnings?

Knockout Picks is required to report any winnings of $600 (net of entry fees) or more for each individual to the Internal Revenue Service on Form 1099-MISC. Knockout Picks may request specific information from the winners prior to crediting their account with the winnings, this information will be used to report the necessary winnings in January of the following year. In addition, each January any winner who won $600 or more in the previous year will be asked to provide an updated address and social security number (if not previously provided).

Do I get a Referral Bonus for Guaranteed (gpp) Leagues?

You will receive a referral bonus from players that you refer that:

  • Enters and plays in any Leagues/Tournament that has an entry fee and does not cancel.
  • Enters and plays in any Guaranteed Leagues/Tournament that has an entry fee ONLY if the league fills.

You will not receive a referral bonus from players that you refer that play in Freeroll Leagues/Tournaments or in any League/Tournament that does not have a U.S. Dollar entry fee.

How Do I Make a Deposit/Withdrawal?

You can enter the cashier section of the site by going to the My Account page on the top menu of our homepage. Depositing by PayPal or credit card is an easy process that we have worked to make as simple as possible for both easy deposits and quick, automatic withdrawals.

The steps in withdrawing funds are just as easy. Simply enter the cashier section and click the ‘Withdraw Funds’ button! We offer PayPal or check withdrawals for our players to receive any or all of the cash in their account.

How Do I Know My Money is Safe?

Knockout Picks uses the most secure transaction channels available, guaranteeing members a safe and secure experience.

Trouble Depositing?

The following states do not allow fantasy sports competition for cash: Arizona, Arkansas, Delaware, Iowa, Louisiana, Michigan, Montana, Tennessee, and Washington.

Residents of Alabama, Nebraska, and British Columbia must be 19 years of age to play in Daily Fantasy Sports contests. Residents of Prince Edward Island and Quebec can only participate in free games for Knockout Points (not cash).

If you fall outside these areas and are still having trouble depositing, or have deposit questions in general please don't hesitate to Contact Us.

What are Knockout Points?

Knockout Points can be used to enter certain Knockout Points freerolls, win prizes or buy tickets to various tournaments.

What Happens in the Event of a Tied Contest?

Tied contests will result in the tied players splitting their finishing position's prize money. In head to head contests, tied scores will result in a refund of the contest entry. For more information about tie breakers, please click here.

What Happens if a Game is Cancelled or Rained Out?

If a game is cancelled for any reason including a rain out, the contestant will receive a 0 in the stat column for any players they had in their lineup from that game. Various sites are available to gain updates on both weather and/or a Minnesota roof collapse.
